Attributes

Attributes are passive buffs that are provided by Attribute Shards. Similar to Enchantments, they can either increase Stats or grant other bonuses, such as increased Skill XP, Health regeneration, and more. Attribute Shards are mainly obtained from Hunting, but can also be obtained in Kuudra's Lair.

Obtaining

Attributes can be obtained by Hunting mobs, or from rewards for defeating Kuudra in Kuudra's Hollow. Attributes can also be obtained through Attribute Fusion by combining two types of shards together.

Upgrading

Attributes can be upgraded up to 10 tiers.
The level of your attributes is determined by the amount of Attribute Shards you've syphoned of that particular type. The amount required to upgrade each attribute tier depends on the attribute's rarity, as follows:
